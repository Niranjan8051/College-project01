About

The Institutes are approved by AICTE & Govt. of Madhya Pradesh and having NAAC Accreditation.Initially popular for its Technical education, the IES Group has grown and diversified to meet the changing needs of time and education, with the introduction of courses in Commerce, Teacher Education and Management to meet the educational demand in the above fields.

Our College

IES Group of Institutions is located at Kalkheda, Ratibad main road in Bhopal at a distance of 16 km from Bhopal Airport, 14 km from Bhopal Railway station and 7 km from New Market. Local bus transport system connects the sprawling area of 50 acres lush green campus to the Main City. The verdant ambiance has been felicitated as the first "Green Building Campus of MP" by IGBC, Hyderabad. It also obtained the privilege of being the first Tobacco Free Campus of the State by M.P. Government

The Technical Institute has completed one rewarding Decade in 2017. In this duration, the institute gained ranking among the renowned institutes in the nation and was awarded for many of its regular as well as unique initiatives. Several batches of students achieved their goals by obtaining outstanding results, winning competitions at the national level and landing coveted jobs in leading MNCs. The Management and faculties feel fulfilled and motivated by growth of the institute and the faith shown in them by students, their families and the society at large.
